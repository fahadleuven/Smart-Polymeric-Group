Fatma Nalan Cetin Pontillo

Nalan obtained a double Bachelor’s degree in Materials Science & Nanotechnology and Molecular Biology, Genetics & Bioengineering along with a minor degree in Chemistry from Sabancı University, Turkey.

In 2022 she obtained her Master’s degree in Materials Engineering & Nanotechnology with a speacialization track in Polymer Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, Italy.

In her master thesis, she investigated the effect of the ionic strength and the polymer concentration on the rheological properties of sodium alginate-based hydrogels developed for in vitro mucus models.

Currently, Nalan is doing her joint PhD affiliated with Ghent University and KU Leuven investigating smart polymer-based advanced wound healing materials.

In 2024, she obtained an FWO-SB grant for her project proposal titled ‘Infection-triggered dermal matrix serving chronic wound healing purposes’.

In the scope of this project, she has been working on design and synthesis of multi stimuli-responsive nanoparticle systems for the smart delivery of therapeutics and diagnostic compounds.
