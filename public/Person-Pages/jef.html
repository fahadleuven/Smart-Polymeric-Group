Jef Brebels

Jef Brebels earned his master's degree in Chemical Engineering at KU Leuven's Faculty of Engineering Technology, focusing on the characterization and quantification of precious metals in pharmaceutical waste streams through a thesis in collaboration with Indaver, Antwerp.

While completing his master's, his interests shifted toward the biomedical field. He is now pursuing a PhD at KU Leuven, funded by FWO, under Prof. Arn Mignon and Prof. Veerle Bloemen.

His research investigates enzyme-responsive polymer-based tubular constructs for flexor tendon repair. Combining expertise in polymer synthesis and characterization, he explores electrospinning and melt electrowriting to develop biological and mechanical repair strategies.
